Technical Missions

Our permanent contacts with well established and internationally renowned companies have led us to organize technical missions to many of them both in Europe as well as in Asia and the Americas.

These missions provided our clients with a unique opportunity to get in touch with the world’s “best practices” in their own field of activity.

We have already organized many technical missions allowing more than 100 Top and Middle Management professionals to benefit from a direct contact with some of the world’s most valuable business experiences in many different sectors:

  • Industry (Processing Industry, Agriculture and Mining)
  • Utilities (Energy, Gas and Water)
  • Health (Hospitals)
  • Services (Public Administration)
